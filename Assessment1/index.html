Chicken

The chicken is a bird that people all over the world raise for its meat, eggs, and feathers. It belongs to the group of domesticated, or tame, birds called poultry. Chickens have short wings and a heavy body.

A fish is an aquatic creature that lives in the water for its whole life. Fish have fins that help them swim in the water. They are found in rivers, ponds, oceans, and many other water bodies. Small fish feed on sea plants while big fish eat these small fish for survival.

Egg

People have eaten eggs for thousands of years. There are many types of egg, but the most common choice is that of the chicken. Eggs contain several vitamins and minerals that are essential parts of a healthful diet. In many parts of the world, eggs are a readily available, inexpensive food.
